Role & Responsibilities

Are you passionate about cars and providing exceptional customer service? We're looking for a skilled Service Advisor to join our team and help us deliver top-notch service to our valued customers.

  • Greet customers and assess their service needs.
  • Schedule appointments and manage service appointments efficiently.
  • Provide accurate estimates for repairs and maintenance services.
  • Communicate effectively with technicians and customers throughout the service process.
  • Ensure customer satisfaction by addressing any concerns or questions promptly.
  • Maintain detailed records of service transactions and customer interactions.

Requirements

  • Previous experience as a Service Advisor or similar role in the automotive industry preferred.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Excellent customer service abilities.
  • Proficiency in automotive service software preferred.
  • Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment.
  • High school diploma or equivalent required; additional automotive training or certification a plus.

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for career growth and advancement.
  • Training and development programs.
  • Employee discounts on products and services.
  • Friendly and supportive work environment.

Working Days

  • Weekly: 5 days.
  • Weekend: Saturday, Sunday
